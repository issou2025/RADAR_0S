## Descrição da vaga

Role Overview

The UX Designer will own the end-to-end design of features across our three product surfaces — platform, agent, and web portal — turning complex business and technical challenges into intuitive, responsive user experiences. This is a hands-on, generalist role for someone who thrives in the fast-paced, high-uncertainty environment of an early-stage B2B SaaS product. You will design from sketches and wireframes through high-fidelity prototypes and developer-ready specs, help us build our first Design System from the ground up, and support discovery alongside the team when opportunities arise. In this position, you will work closely with our UX Lead and partner directly with the PM (who is also our CTO)

 
Responsibilities

Design end-to-end: from initial sketches and wireframes to high-fidelity, responsive prototypes across the platform, agent, and web portal.
Produce high-fidelity screens and detailed specifications in Figma (English and pt-BR), ensuring the development team has everything needed for precise implementation.
Help build and evolve BotCity's first Design System — components, patterns, and tokens — in partnership with the UX Lead and the front-end team.
Analyze existing functionalities and propose UX improvements to reduce friction and enhance user efficiency.
Build interactive prototypes to test usability and communicate complex flows to stakeholders.
Support discovery alongside the UX Lead and the PM: run qualitative interviews, map user journeys, and validate hypotheses and prototypes with B2B clients when needed.
Transform findings into clear UX artifacts (User Journey Maps, Personas) and documented insights shared with the team.
Partner with Product, Engineering, and Customer Success to ensure design solutions align with technical constraints and business goals.
