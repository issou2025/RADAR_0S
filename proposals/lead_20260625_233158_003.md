What is CAD?

Computer-Aided Design (CAD) is a software-based technology used to create, modify, analyze, and optimize designs. Instead of traditional hand drafting, CAD allows professionals to produce highly accurate 2D drawings and 3D models efficiently.

CAD is widely used in industries such as:

Civil Engineering
Architecture
Mechanical Engineering
Interior Design
Construction
Manufacturing
Product Design
Automotive Industry
Industrial Engineering

Today, companies prefer professionals who are skilled in industry-standard CAD software because it improves productivity, minimizes errors, and enhances design quality. CAD software such as AutoCAD, Revit, SolidWorks, CATIA, and Civil 3D are among the most in-demand tools across engineering and design sectors.
