## Accepted architecture principles

1. Source CAD/BIM geometry and approved project state are authoritative; generated images and meshes are derivatives.
2. Prefer deterministic converters, scripts and explicit schemas over free-form model mutation.
3. Use provider-neutral interfaces for image generation and external AI services.
4. Default to read-only inspection, dry-run, diff, approval, bounded transaction and rollback.
5. Every reusable automation must have inputs, rules, allowed actions, validation and expected output.
6. Quantities require named calculation rules and provenance, not raw property export alone.
7. Successful generated automation may become a registered DIOS/Skeleton capability only after review and tests.
8. External AI services must not receive private project/customer data unless the operator explicitly approves a compliant route.
9. Visual quality and geometric correctness are separate validation dimensions.
10. AI should route work to specialized CAD/BIM tools rather than attempting to synthesize every object itself.
