﻿# Family Expenses — Project Plan

## Context

Roman and his wife Svetlana currently track family expenses in a Google Sheet:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1xglE6i5nID0A3v8HfEGDfeoiburK08EA_NwlBakmlls/edit?gid=421526018#gid=421526018

The Google Sheet is private (requires sign-in). **Dependency:** Roman needs to share the sheet or describe its structure.

## Problem

Google Sheets is inconvenient for quick expense entry, especially from mobile. No receipt scanning. No categorization workflow. No modern UI.

## Vision

A family expense tracking system with:
1. **Web admin panel** — dashboard, expense entry, categories, reports, admin settings
2. **Android mobile app** — modern, quality, easy expense entry from phone
3. **Receipt scanning** — photo of receipt → app asks whose expense + category → extracts data → saves receipt image
4. **Free hosting** — must be free tier, no monthly costs

## Expected Outcomes

- [ ] Web admin panel deployed and accessible
- [ ] Android app installable (APK or Play Store)
- [ ] Receipt scanning with OCR
- [ ] Multi-user (Roman + Svetlana)
- [ ] Category-based tracking
- [ ] Data export (CSV/Excel)
- [ ] Modern, clean, easy-to-use UI
