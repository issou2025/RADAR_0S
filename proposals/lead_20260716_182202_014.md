**Trash surface: view and restore discarded Outcomes**

*Project description:*
## User Story

As a user, I want to see the Outcomes I've discarded and restore them if I change my mind, so trashing feels safe and reversible — which keeps me willing to keep my lists clean.

## Context

`CONTEXT.md` defines the **Trash** implicit List (`Intent = trash`, regardless of Completion) and notes its *"user-facing surface deferred"*. There was no tracking issue for that deferral until now. Per the model, Done and Trash are disjoint — a trashed-and-completed Outcome surfaces in Trash only.

## Scope

- Trash list view: all Outcomes with `intent = 'trash'`, newest-trashed first
- Restore action: set Intent back to `next` or `maybe` (user choice), which re-projects the Outcome onto its Lists — no membership rows to move
- Entry point per the epic-level design pass on #34 (likely drawer, alongside Done)
- Restoring stamps `last_clarified_at` (trashing/untrashing is a clarification act per `CONTEXT.md`)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Trash lists exactly the `Intent = trash` Outcomes, including completed-then-trashed ones (which do not appear in Done)
- [ ] Restore returns the Outcome to the chosen Intent and it immediately reappears on the corresponding List
- [ ] Restore stamps `last_clarified_at`
- [ ] No physical deletion anywhere in this story (data-integrity rule: rows persist; Intent expresses the stance)

## Out of Scope

- Permanent purge / "empty trash" — deliberately excluded until retention needs are clear (never-destructive default)
