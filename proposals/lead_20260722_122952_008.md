> From **31 Aug 2026**, if your target API level is not within one year of the latest Android release, you won't be able to update your app.

After that date we can no longer ship updates to Play until the app targets API 36.

## Android 16 behaviour changes to check

Applies once `targetSdk = 36`:

- **Edge-to-edge is mandatory** — `Window.setDecorFitsSystemWindows(false)` is forced and the opt-out (`windowOptOutEdgeToEdgeEnforcement`) is ignored. Highest-risk item for us: check that the barcode screen, charts, and bottom nav are not drawn under the status/navigation bars. May need `SystemChrome.setEnabledSystemUIMode` / `SafeArea` fixes.
- **Predictive back** enabled by default — verify back navigation and any `WillPopScope`/`PopScope` usage still behaves.
- **Large-screen / orientation restrictions ignored** on displays ≥600dp — the app can be resized/rotated even if the manifest locks orientation. Check layouts don't break in landscape/tablet.
- **Stricter JobScheduler & background work quotas** — low risk, we have no foreground services (manifest declares only INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, CAMERA).
- **Camera / media permission changes** — barcode scanner uses CAMERA; verify the permission flow on Android 16.
