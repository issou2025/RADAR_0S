**App Philosophy & Core Objective:**
To solve the issue of "routine boredom" and habituation faced by busy users with traditional apps, by introducing a calm, seamless, and completely non-intrusive experience.

**Core Features & Architectural Design:**
1. **Intriguing Random Appearance**: The remembrance does not appear every single time the phone is unlocked to avoid routine. Instead, it displays at random intervals, keeping the user in eager anticipation.
2. **True Dark Mode**: A 100% pitch-black interface with thin, dim gray fonts. This ensures easy readability at night or work without increasing screen brightness or drawing attention.
3. **Silent Tapping Integration**: Users can perform Tasbih directly on the lock screen widget with subtle haptic feedback to confirm the count, without unlocking the phone or making any sound.
4. **The 30-Day Competitive Garden (The Flywheel Effect)**: The user starts with a small, dimly lit seed. Every lock-screen Dhikr provides "warm light particles." Over 30 days, it evolves into a luminous, fruit-bearing tree. At the end of the month, the fruits drop to sprout new trees, flowers, and rivers—visually reflecting multiplied rewards while fostering long-term engagement and spiritual competition.
