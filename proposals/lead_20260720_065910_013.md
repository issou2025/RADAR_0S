### What happened?

In my Music Assistant settings I am connected to my Spotify account and filesystem (remote share) but for my user I have selected in user management to only see music from filesystem (remote share). When looking at the album list, I see all the albums on my hard drive (as expected) but when I look at individual albums I also see tracks from (I'm assuming) my Spotify liked songs.

It doesn't happen with every liked track from albums I also have on my hard drive, but maybe that's because they're not recognized as the same album?

### How to reproduce

Go to Music Assistant -> Settings -> User management -> pick a user -> select 'Filesystem (remote share)' and unselect Spotify.

Then go to albums and select some albums from your hard drive from which you know you also have liked songs on Spotify.
